Kramdown Uses Double Dollar Signs Even In Inline Math Mode

18 Oct 2019- Zhiqiang Zang

Kramdown uses double $ even for inline math mode. If you want a displayed mode, please surround your math block with blank lines.

See https://kramdown.gettalong.org/syntax.html#math-blocks

See https://github.com/gettalong/kramdown/issues/627
